The Witch Queen

Who is the Witch Queen?

The Witch Queen, also known by the name Savathun, is one of the three primal gods of the Hive race in the world of Destiny. Having been teased since 2015 during the Taken King expansion, where players fought and killed her brother, Oryx, long time Destiny fans should be excited to finally clash with such a legendary enemy.

""Who is Savathûn?" you ask. You already know, oh Guardian mine. I am your friend."— Savathûn to the Guardian

The Story so Far...

Timeline of Events

  1. Crota, son of Oryx is killed on Earth's moon
  2. Oryx arrives in our solar System outside of Saturn
  3. Oryx is killed aboard his ship, after wreaking havoc in the asteroid belt
  4. Years later, the Dreaming City is discovered in the asteroid belt
  5. It is revealed that Savathun and Oryx had taken control of the Dreaming City in secret during Oryx's arrival
  6. The Queen of the asteroid belt wages war against Savathun's army
  7. Savathun's curse remains, trapping the Dreamin City in an endless loop
  8. A new threat approaches the solar system, provoking Savathun to act

In the most recent story content available from Destiny, Savathun had infiltrated the ranks of the heroes are players have been fighting alongside under the guise of Osiris, a guardian of humanity that we had been made to beleive had narrowly escaped death investigating Hive activity on Earth's moon. It was recently revealed that he had been captured and replaced by Savathun so she could use Osiris as a bargaining chip to strike a deal. As it turns out, the third and youngest primal god of the Hive, Xivu Arath, has begun a war against her sister. Savathun has asked for players assistance in escaping her sister in exchange for the safe return og Osiris. After the end of the most current season of Destiny, we saw Savathun escape and keep her promise to return Osiris, but she escaped from us as well.

What is to Come

With what is known about the upcoming Witch Queen expansion, we see that Savathun has stolen the power of Light, the power source our players use to fight against the enemy factions attacking our solar system. For the first time in Destiny's history, players will be forced to fight enemies wielding the same power we have been using against them for over seven years. In addition to this new story, we have many new features added to the game, promising an all new experience to the game.

New Features Coming to Witch Queen

A new legendary difficulty for the campaign
A standard campaign will be avialable
Completeing the campaign on legendary difficulty will award players with higher level weapons and armor
A complete rework of the old void elemental subclasses
Void subclasses are being changed to operate closer to the recently added stasis subclass for more customization options
A new weapon crafting system
A system that will allow players to build weapons with levels of cutomization that were previously unobtainable
A new archetype of weapon called the "glaive"
A new melee weapon to give an alternate option to swords
Savathun's Throne World: A new destination to explore
Savathun's dimension will be accessible, the first time a Hive god's throne world has had this level of exploration available
A brand new raid for players to test their skills in
Much of what this raid contains is still a mystery, but it will certainly boast incredible endgame challenges for players to face

What Does it All Mean?

Destiny's yearly expansions often boast a massive update consisting of weapon and ability balancing as well as the addition of hours upon hours of new content to explore. Witch Queen is launching after a year in Destiny that focused on story development in a way destiny has not done before, and when the expansion launches, players will finally be able to see the fruits of their labor.
